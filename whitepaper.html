01 / INTRODUCTION & EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The Decentralized Finance (DeFi) ecosystem on the Solana network is experiencing exponential growth, driven by ultra-fast transaction speeds and minimal gas fees. However, market participants frequently face two critical vectors of friction: predatory bot manipulation (MEV) on decentralized exchanges and unsustainable, hyper-inflationary reward tokenomics.

Provizto introduces a secure, unified DeFi ecosystem designed to neutralize these inefficiencies. By seamlessly integrating an MEV-protected AMM DEX, an Automated Yield Optimizer, and a sustainable "Real Yield" revenue-sharing mechanism, Provizto establishes a fair, resilient, and highly lucrative investment landscape for $VZT token holders.

03 / REVENUE MODEL & DISTRIBUTION ARCHITECTURE

Provizto charges a standard 0.3% protocol fee on all asset swaps processed through its AMM DEX. These generated fees are automatically aggregated and split by smart contracts using the following transparent allocation:

Total Fee Swapped = 0.3% × Transaction Volume
  • 50% of Fees (0.15% of Volume) → VZT Lockers Pool (Real Yield)
  • 30% of Fees (0.09% of Volume) → Liquidity Providers (LP) Rewards
  • 15% of Fees (0.045% of Volume) → Platform Treasury & Development
  • 5% of Fees (0.015% of Volume) → On-Chain Affiliate System
